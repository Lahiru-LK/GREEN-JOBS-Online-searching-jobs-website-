A true pioneer in digital recruiting, GREENJOBS was founded in 2022 to bring talent and companies together. Now, 8 resumes are uploaded and 64 job search queries are entered on Monster every minute.1although it’s comparable in quality and usability to Indeed, it has fewer job search filters and not as many opportunities.

Like Indeed, GREENJOBS caters to job seekers from all experience levels and work styles (freelance, temp, part-time, full-time, etc.) and its job search tools are free to use. You need to create an account using your email address in order to apply to some job listing on GREENJOBS , but it takes only little time to do so. Jobs that link to another website for applying don't require an account.

Once you have an account, you’ll be able to save job positions and search queries as well as sign up for email alerts when new jobs are added in the fields you’re interested in. Candidates can search jobs by location, company, title, but there is no option to search by salary or experience level. In addition to its job search function, GREENJOBS also provides salary research and comparison tools and offers makeovers for premium resumes,
