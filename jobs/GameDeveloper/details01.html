Job Description


Responsibilities

  • Does extensive market research and dissection of game products and potential game mechanics.
  • Working alongside other game designers to assist in the creative processes of game ideas and problem-solving.
  • Design fair and balanced math models, systems/content based on the overall game creative direction to meet the KPI requirements;
  • Create effective documentation for all systems designed in a clear manner & make it understandable for the entire development team;
  • Maintain a data-driven design methodology for systems/content design through careful analysis of user feedback and key metrics to adjust gameplay and balance;
  • Collaborate effectively with other departments of the development team (Tech, Art, QA, Product, etc) to create the best product possible;

Requirements:

  • Degree in Game Design, Computer Science or any relevant field.
  • 1-2 year(s) working experience or have shipped at least 1-2 game(s) as a designer or relevant fields.
  • A proactive team player who can communicate clearly and constructively.
  • Capable of working in a dynamic environment with shifting priorities.
  • Knowledgeable in math concepts such as Probability and Statistics.
  • High level of competency in Microsoft Office / Google Workspace software tools, especially Microsoft Excel / Google Sheets;
  • Ability to create detailed and effective documentation as well as basic mock-ups based on UX design theories;
  • Comprehensive knowledge and a deep passion for games across multiple genres of PC/mobile games and relevant game design theories;

Bonus Points:

  • Experience working with game engines such as Cocos Creator and Unity;
  • Experience working with agile production methodologies and relevant project management tools such as JIRA;
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills in English.
