Job Description


Job Description

  • Takes charge of the planning, development and management of game system design, game balance, the overall player experience and gameplay cycles for a yet-to-be-released online racing management game project which has a general game system defined, but is undergoing development.


Key Responsibilities

  • A lead game design role responsible for planning game design and player experience for an upcoming online game.
  • The hired Game Design Lead will take an active role in refining existing game design, and contribute to the development and improvement of the game systems to maximize the long term sustainability and growth of the game, and maximizing satisfaction and participation of users, and consequently the lifespan of the game.
  • The main activities will be researching and analyzing competitor game systems and their strengths and weaknesses, understanding player-base expectations and requirements, and applying knowledge and findings to optimize game systems to achieve the desired outcomes. A direct channel will exist between the job holder and management, to discuss proposed game design directions and their expected outcomes.

    • Objectives:


    1. To own, develop and maintain game system design to ensure consistent achievement of targets with the player base.
    2. The main objective of this role, in simple terms, is to make sure the game systems keep the player base happy across short, medium and long terms.
    3. KPIs will be satisfaction of the overall player base, interpreted through player base growth, retention and activity levels.

Core Skills

  • Online/mobile game design fundamentals
  • Game design background expected
  • Understanding of player behavior and psychology
  • Game currency/item economies and sustainability
  • Balance of competitive games

Requirements

  • Singaporeans are welcomed.
  • have at least 3 years of working experience in the role.
