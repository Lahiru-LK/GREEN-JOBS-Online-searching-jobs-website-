Job Description


Job Responsibilities:

  • Develop, execute and oversee procedures, policies and related training plans for network & security administration.
  • Update and follow-up on cybersecurity advisory from threat intelligence.
  • Conduct periodic tests on security controls to identify and rectify gaps.
  • Deploy and perform a periodic vulnerability assessment of IT assets and projects.
  • Liaise with managed security providers, vendors and infrastructure technical staff on security solutions.
  • Manage and provide remedial actions to security incidents.
  • Ensure all security solutions are functional and comply with policies and procedures.
  • Manage hardware equipment, virtualized environment, servers and network.
  • Manage and establish priorities for maintenance, design, development and analysis of network equipment including firewall, routers, proxy servers, switches, WAN accelerators, DNS, and DHCP.
  • Ensure network and security equipment are updated to the latest firmware.
  • Documentation of network and security policies.
  • Manager and facilitate periodic disaster recovery exercises.
  • Conduct periodic cybersecurity awareness programs.
  • Oversee operational costs, conduct near along with long-term financial estimates for expanded functionality and user base.
  • Direct and administer conditional network engineers to provide leadership and direction.
  • Ensure to practice IT asset management inclusive of component inventory maintenance and associated documentation.
  • Negotiate with outsourcers, vendors and contractors for infrastructure-specific products and services secure.

Job Requirements:

  • Degree in Computer or Network/Cybersecurity-related Major Professional Certificate.
  • Have a strong understanding of IT infrastructure and security operations.
  • 5 years of working experience in leading, implementing and administrating Vmware/Hyper-V, network devices such as switches, routers, firewalls, wireless controllers, and wireless access points.
  • Good understanding of network systems, network architecture, routing, switching, firewall, and security.
  • Good knowledge and hands-on experience managing network security, server security, application security, endpoint security, email security, physical access security, logical access security, etc.
  • Hands-on experience in security monitoring, network diagnostic, network analytics and network security devices.
  • Experience in Veeam backup solution.
  • Experience in disaster recovery concepts and practices.
  • Experience in a hybrid-cloud environment and GCP/AWS is an advantage.
  • Experience in web hosting, web proxy and reverse proxy.
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical skills as well as excellent interpersonal and communication skills.
