Job Description

About the Role

An established and fast growing technology firm with global presence, looking for an experienced Infrastructure Project Manager with experience in technology and/or FSI sectors. As part of the team, you will be responsible to handle the infrastructure projects for the entire group within the regions. You will report directly to the Head of Department.


Job Description

  • Responsible for handling network and server infrastructure projects (new office set up, office relocation, and tech refresh projects).
  • Work closely with internal and external business stakeholders to understand and analyse business requirements.
  • Translate business IT requirements to technical requirements and propose full-scale infrastructure project proposal with the technical team.
  • Define resource allocation, work scope and responsibilities of assignee and project timeline.
  • Drive project direction and actively monitor project status to ensure high quality of project delivery.
  • Define and address problem encountered and decide alternatives/work-around in a timely manner.
  • Ensure UAT acceptable from project personnel and report to stakeholders on project outcomes.
  • Create and maintain records of project documentations.
  • Responsible for any other ad hoc duties assigned.

Requirements

  • Diploma/ Bachelor in Computer Science /Information Technology/ Science/ Mathematics or relevant field.
  • At least 5 years of experience in handling Infrastructure Projects for FSI or Technology companies.
  • Experience in delivering high quality and successful infrastructure project delivery experience.
  • Strong experience in Network and System solutions related projects.
  • Proficient in stakeholder management with strategic mindset to advise internal and external stakeholders.
  • Strong management, coordination, and problem-solving skills.
  • A desire to thrive in a hard-working, fast-paced and collaborative team.
  • Enthusiasm for learning and utilising new technology.
  • Great communication skills and customer centric.
  • A motivated mind-set, someone who's great to have as a team-mate.
