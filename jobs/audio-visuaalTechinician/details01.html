Job Description


  • Operate and run day to day AV and VC system for virtual and physical events
  • Plan and manage schedule for multiple Teams/ VC meeting to ensure setup and tests are done
  • Responsible in ensuring AV and VC system are in good working condition by conducting predictive and preventive maintenance

  • Monitor and attend to maintenance service requests

  • Good communication and interpersonal skills, with an eye for detail
  • Well knowledge on MS Teams and Polycom system
  • Positive working attitude
  • Able to handle stress and multi-tasking
  • Good team player
