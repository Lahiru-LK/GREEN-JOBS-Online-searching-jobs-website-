Visual Merchandiser – Design & Retail
Basic: $3,000
Min. Experience: 2 - 3 years
5 days work week
Excellent opportunities and friendly working environment


This is a full time design role supporting our retail display and product design team. We are looking for creative talents with strong design thinking & graphic design skills to create eye-catching and innovative visual displays to elevate the brand's retail experience.


Responsibilities and duties:

  • Creating and implementing a visual merchandising strategy for Play Nation’s stores and display shelves at our retail partners premises, including supermarkets and convenience stores.
  • Designing Pop-up, retail shelves, exhibition and in-store brand experiences.
  • Ensuring designs are in line with the Play Nation brand, products, and target market.
  • Generating sketches for visual displays according to our visual design requirements.
  • Finalizing designs and creating detailed 2D / 3D mock ups for presentation to buyers / clients.
  • Sourcing the props, creative materials, and accessories for shelve displays.
  • Installing and setting up Pop-Up, retail shelf, and in-store displays.
  • Assisting with changing of displays for new product launches, promotions or to reflect festive or seasonal themes.
  • Ensuring uniformity of displays across multiple store locations.
  • Liaising with other retail professionals, including buyers, marketing executives, and retail merchandisers, when necessary.
